### Analysis
Pass 56 lazy loader (index.html:1110) marks these as lazy for progressive loading.
But SW PRECACHE pre-caches them unconditionally, defeating the lazy strategy.
Net effect: these 4 assets load eagerly via SW, lazy loader logic is bypassed.
